Our Story

Nestled in the shadows of the majestic Himalayas, where prayer flags flutter in the mountain breeze and the scent of spices fills the air, lies a cozy pizzeria unlike any other. Himalayan Crust Pizzeria draws inspiration from the rich culinary heritage of Nepal, blending traditional flavors with the beloved Italian classic.

Legend has it that the founder, a seasoned trekker and passionate food enthusiast, embarked on a culinary journey across the rugged terrain of Nepal. Along the way, he encountered the warmth of Nepali hospitality and the bold flavors of the local cuisine. Inspired by the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas and the vibrant culture of Nepal, he returned home with a visionâ€”to create a pizzeria that would capture the essence of the mountains in every bite. Thus, Himalayan Crust Pizzeria was born.

At Himalayan Crust, pizzas are crafted with care, using locally sourced ingredients that reflect the diverse landscapes of Nepal. From the spicy heat of Himalayan chilies to the earthy richness of yak cheese, each topping tells a story of the land.
As word of mouth spread, Himalayan Crust Pizzeria became a beloved gathering spot for locals and travelers alike, a place where weary adventurers could refuel with a slice of comfort and warmth amidst the grandeur of the Himalayas.

Today, Himalayan Crust continues to honor its roots, serving up delicious pizzas infused with the spirit of Nepal. With every bite, diners embark on a culinary journey through the mountains, savoring the flavors of a land where every meal is a celebration of life.

Some of the Pizza that you can enjoy at Himalayan Crust Pizza includes:

  • Margherita:A classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Pepperoni:A popular choice featuring tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of pepperoni sausage.
  • Hawaiian:Controversial to some, beloved by others, this pizza typically includes tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham or Canadian bacon, and pineapple.
  • Meat Lover's:Packed with various types of meats like pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and sometimes even meatballs or ham, along with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
